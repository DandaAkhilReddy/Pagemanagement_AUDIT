Executive Summary

Comprehensive Revenue Cycle Management Audit - Page Management Associates

Audit Overview

This comprehensive audit of Page Management Associates' revenue cycle management processes was conducted in November 2024 to identify operational inefficiencies, automation opportunities, and process improvements. The audit covered end-to-end billing operations from patient encounter through payment posting.

Key Objectives

  • Document complete revenue cycle workflows
  • Identify bottlenecks and pain points
  • Quantify operational inefficiencies
  • Propose automation solutions
  • Establish performance baselines

Scope of Review

The audit encompassed all major RCM functions including:

Front-End Operations

  • Patient registration
  • Insurance verification
  • Census processing
  • Charge capture

Mid-Cycle Management

  • Claim creation
  • Coding accuracy
  • Claim transmission
  • Clearinghouse processing

Back-End Collections

  • Payment posting
  • Denial management
  • AR follow-up
  • Financial reconciliation

Strategic Recommendations

1. Immediate Actions (0-2 weeks)

  • Deploy duplicate detection logic
  • Clear posting backlog with overtime/temps
  • Establish daily reconciliation routine

2. Short-term Improvements (2-8 weeks)

  • Automate EOB-to-bank matching
  • Implement payer lag monitoring
  • Create centralized issue tracking system

3. Long-term Transformation (2-6 months)

  • Full RPA for payment posting
  • AI-powered denial prediction
  • Real-time dashboard implementation
