Humana - Special Focus

New Medical Records Process (October 2024 Change)

30-Day Pending Letter Scheme:

  • Step 1: Humana requests medical records
  • Step 2: Isaiah uploads records + sends invoice ($2/page per FL statute)
  • Step 3: Humana auto-generates "pending/finding" letter
  • Step 4: Adds 30 more days to payment timeline
  • Impact: Payment delays increased by 30+ days
  • Counter-measure: Track and enforce interest on late payments

  • Track invoice numbers
  • 30-day payment terms
  • Interest calculation if late

Commercial Payer Patterns

Blue Cross Blue Shield

Processing Characteristics:

  • 1-day payment lag (very predictable)
  • Prior authorization required for many services
  • Multiple plan types (HMO, PPO, EPO)
  • Different processing by state
  • Good electronic capabilities

United Healthcare / Optum

Processing Characteristics:

  • Variable payment timing
  • Shows as "Optum" in bank deposits
  • Complex authorization requirements
  • Multiple product lines
  • $32,000 approved today (example)

Aetna

Processing Characteristics:

  • Pre-notification of payment dates
  • Generally reliable timing
  • CVS Health integration
  • Good portal access
  • Clear EOB documentation

Medicaid Replacement Plans

Common Issues

Coverage Confusion:

  • Wrong plan billed: Traditional vs. replacement
  • Hospice override: Hospice takes precedence
  • Eligibility changes: Monthly updates needed
  • Authorization delays: Hospital-side failures
  • Network issues: In/out of network confusion
