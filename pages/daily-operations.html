Daily Operations

Operational Cadence, Workflows, and Report Management

Daily Schedule Overview

New Operating Cadence (Effective November 1, 2024)

AM
Morning Session
AR Follow-ups & Collections

Afternoon Session

Previous Schedule Issues (Pre-November)

Old Weekly Cadence Problems:

  • Monday-Tuesday: Posting only - created AR buildup
  • Wednesday-Thursday: AR work only - posting backlog grew
  • Friday: Self-pay + posting - inconsistent coverage
  • Result: Uneven AR swings and cash flow disruption

Morning Operations (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

8:00 AM - Census Review

Activities:

  • Receive daily census from facilities (PDF format)
  • Review new admissions and discharges
  • Compare with yesterday's census for changes
  • Identify potential duplicates before entry
8:30 AM - Patient Registration

Activities:

  • Enter new patients from census
  • Verify insurance eligibility
  • Update demographics for existing patients
  • Flag accounts needing additional information
9:00 AM - AR Work Begins

Activities:

  • Review aging reports by payer
  • Priority: 60-90 day accounts
  • Call insurance for claim status
  • Submit medical records as requested
  • Appeal denials with documentation
10:00 AM - Denial Management

Activities:

  • Review new denials from overnight ERA
  • Categorize by denial reason
  • Route to appropriate team member
  • Initiate appeals for medical necessity
11:00 AM - Report Generation

Activities:

  • Pull AR aging reports from AdvancedMD
  • Generate daily collection priorities
  • Update tracking spreadsheets
  • Prepare SFTP drops for assignments

Afternoon Operations (12:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

12:00 PM - ERA Download

Activities:

  • Download morning ERA/EOB files
  • Import 835 files into AdvancedMD
  • Print posting worksheets
  • Identify any missing batches
1:00 PM - Payment Posting Begins

Activities:

  • Post Medicare payments first (highest volume)
  • Process commercial insurance ERAs
  • Apply contractual adjustments (CO45)
  • Transfer patient responsibilities
  • Target: Post $100,000+ daily
3:00 PM - Secondary Claims

Activities:

  • Generate secondary claims from primary EOBs
  • Attach primary EOB documentation
  • Submit to secondary insurance
  • Track crossover claims
4:00 PM - Claim Transmission

Activities:

  • Manual transmission batch (886 claims example)
  • Review transmission acknowledgments
  • Address any rejection notices
  • Prepare for 7 PM auto-transmission
5:00 PM - Daily Wrap-up

Activities:

  • Update posting totals in tracker
  • Note unfinished items for tomorrow
  • Send daily summary to leadership
  • Set priorities for next day
