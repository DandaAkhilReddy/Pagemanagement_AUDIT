Detailed Process Steps

Step 1: Patient Encounter & Documentation

Provider Actions:

  • Provider sees patient at facility
  • Documents encounter in EMR
  • Enters charges in IngeniousMed app
  • Selects appropriate CPT codes
  • Adds diagnosis codes (minimum required)

Common Issues:

  • Providers creating manual patients due to sync lag
  • Incomplete documentation
  • Missing modifiers (especially Q6 for locum)
  • Doctors have until end of month +10 days to complete charges
Step 2: Census Processing & Patient Registration

Daily Census Workflow:

  • Facility provides daily census (PDF format)
  • Staff reviews census for new admissions
  • Manual entry of patient demographics
  • Insurance verification process
  • Adjacent-day census comparison (manual)

Registration Data Points:

  • Patient name, DOB, gender
  • Insurance ID and group numbers
  • Admission/discharge dates
  • Attending physician
  • Facility and room number
Step 3: IngeniousMed → AdvancedMD Synchronization

System Integration:

  • Charge data flows from IM to AMD
  • If doctor bills same day, charges appear immediately in AMD
  • Manual reconciliation required for missing data

Assignment Workflow:

  • IngeniousMed system update: Assignment delayed by 1 day
  • On November 14th, can only assign encounters from November 13th
  • If doctor doesn't bill, Reshma's team works on yesterday's encounters
  • This is an operational workflow issue, not a cause of duplicates

Duplicate Patient Issue:

  • Root Cause: Doctors cannot quickly find existing patients in search
  • Under time pressure, doctors create new patient records instead of thorough searching
  • Staff must identify and merge duplicates (2-3x rework)
Step 4: Claim Review & Coding

Coding Team Actions:

  • Paul and Heather review all claims
  • Verify CPT codes match documentation
  • Add required modifiers (Q6, 25, etc.)
  • Ensure minimum diagnosis codes present
  • Flag non-billable codes
  • Clear review queue daily

Quality Checks:

  • Documentation supports level of service
  • Correct place of service
  • Valid insurance on file
  • No bundling issues
  • Prior authorization when required
Step 5: Claim Transmission

Transmission Schedule:

  • Multiple transmissions throughout day
  • Automatic batch at 7:00 PM nightly
  • 886 claims example transmission at 4 PM
  • Creates 837 electronic claim files

Clearinghouse Processing (McKesson):

  • Receives 837 files from AdvancedMD
  • Validates claim format and data
  • Returns acknowledgment reports
  • Generates exclusion reports for errors
  • Forwards clean claims to payers
Step 6: Payer Adjudication

Payer Processing:

  • Payer receives claim from clearinghouse
  • Eligibility verification
  • Medical necessity review
  • Apply contract rates
  • Calculate allowed amounts
  • Determine patient responsibility

Common Payer Responses:

  • Clean approval with payment
  • Partial payment with adjustments
  • Request for medical records
  • Denial for various reasons
  • Pending for additional information
Step 7: EOB Receipt & Payment

ERA/EOB Processing (835 Files):

  • Electronic remittance advice received
  • Shows payment breakdown by CPT
  • Lists adjustment codes (CO45, etc.)
  • Indicates patient responsibility
  • Provides batch/check numbers

Payment Timeline Variances:

  • Medicare: Often 0-1 day early
  • BCBS: 1-day lag typical
  • Humana: 3-day lag standard
  • Aetna: Pre-notification of payment
  • United/Optum: Variable timing
Step 8: Payment Posting

Posting Process:

  • Afternoon focus (after AM AR work)
  • Match EOB to patient accounts
  • Apply payments by CPT code
  • Post contractual adjustments
  • Transfer patient responsibility
  • Target: $100,000+ daily posting

Posting Challenges:

  • Current backlog: $237,000
  • Manual process for each payment
  • Matching issues with missing EOBs
  • Bank deposits not matching system

Bottleneck Areas

Duplicate Patient Entries (User Error)

  • Doctors cannot quickly locate patients in IngeniousMed search
  • Under time pressure, create new patient records instead of thorough searching
  • Results in 2-3x rework for billing and reconciliation
  • Solution: Improve user training and search interface

Manual Posting Process

  • Each payment posted individually
  • No auto-posting rules
  • $237K current backlog
  • Afternoon-only schedule constraint

Payer Response Delays

  • Humana 3-day payment lag
  • Medical record requests (30-day hold)
  • Prior authorization delays
  • Missing EOBs from payers
